# Fan Engagement

Build and nurture fan relationships through email, segmentation, and community.

## When to Activate

- "Grow my fanbase," "email my fans," "fan engagement"
- "Set up an email list," "segment my audience"
- Release plan triggers fan communication phase

## Fan Tiers

Segment fans by engagement level:

| Tier | Definition | Size | Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Superfans** | Buy merch, attend shows, share everything, reply to DMs | 1-5% | Exclusive access, early releases, personal messages |
| **Active fans** | Stream regularly, engage on social, on email list | 10-20% | Pre-saves, new release alerts, behind-the-scenes |
| **Casual listeners** | Stream occasionally, follow on 1-2 platforms | 30-50% | Broad announcements, viral content, playlist features |
| **Dormant** | Followed once, no recent engagement | 20-40% | Re-engagement campaigns, "miss you" emails, new sound teasers |

## Email Campaigns (Klaviyo)

### List Building

- **Link-in-bio**: Smart link with email capture (linkfire, feature.fm)
- **Pre-save campaigns**: Require email for exclusive content
- **Merch checkout**: Automatic Klaviyo sync from Shopify
- **Live shows**: QR code at merch table → email signup
- **Social CTAs**: "Join my text list" / "Get early access"

### Email Sequences

**Welcome Series (automated):**
1. **Immediately**: "Welcome" + link to latest release + who you are
2. **Day 3**: Behind-the-scenes story + unreleased clip
3. **Day 7**: Exclusive content (demo, acoustic version, photo set)
4. **Day 14**: "Here's what's coming" + invite to follow on social

**Release Campaign:**
1. **T-2 weeks**: "Something's coming" teaser
2. **T-1 week**: Pre-save link + artwork reveal
3. **Release day**: "IT'S OUT" with all streaming links
4. **T+3 days**: "Thank you" + streaming milestone + share CTA
5. **T+2 weeks**: Behind-the-scenes of making the track

**Re-engagement (dormant fans):**
1. "Hey, it's been a while" + best track from last 6 months
2. If opens: follow up with new release
3. If no open after 2 emails: archive (don't delete, just remove from active sends)

### Email Writing Rules

1. **Subject lines**: Short, personal, curiosity-driven. "this one's different" > "NEW SINGLE OUT NOW!!!"
2. **From name**: Artist's first name, not brand name. "Jordan" not "Jordan Smith Music"
3. **Length**: 100-200 words max. Fans skim.
4. **CTA**: One clear action per email. Don't ask them to stream AND buy merch AND follow on TikTok.
5. **Frequency**: Max 2x/month outside of release campaigns. Don't burn the list.
6. **Mobile first**: 70%+ of fans read email on phone. Short paragraphs, big buttons.

## Direct Fan Engagement

### Social Media Engagement Rules

- Reply to **every** comment in the first hour after posting (algorithm boost)
- DM superfans who share your music to stories — "saw you shared [track], means a lot 🙏"
- Like and comment on fan posts that tag you
- Never ignore a fan who made fan art, a cover, or a dance video

### Community Building

- **Discord/Group chat**: For superfans. Exclusive previews, Q&A sessions, polls on artwork/setlists.
- **Close Friends (IG)**: Add superfans. Post demos, raw takes, personal moments.
- **Live sessions**: IG Live, TikTok Live — acoustic performances, Q&A, listening parties.

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Good | Great |
|---|---|---|
| Email open rate | >25% | >40% |
| Email click rate | >3% | >8% |
| Email list growth/month | >5% | >15% |
| IG engagement rate | >3% | >6% |
| TikTok engagement rate | >5% | >10% |
| Fan-to-stream conversion | >10% | >25% |

## The 1,000 True Fans Framework

An artist needs ~1,000 superfans spending $100/year each to earn $100K/year independently.

$100/year = 1 merch item ($35) + 1 vinyl/cassette ($30) + 1 concert ticket ($25) + streaming ($10/year)

Focus on converting casual listeners → active fans → superfans, not on growing raw follower count.
