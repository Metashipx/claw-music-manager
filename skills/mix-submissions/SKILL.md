# Demo & Mix Submissions

Submit music to labels, radio, blogs, YouTube channels, and DJ shows professionally.

## When to Activate

- "Submit to labels," "send my demo," "get on radio"
- "Find blogs for my genre," "submit to YouTube channels"
- "How do I get my track played on [show/station]?"
- Release plan triggers press/submission phase

## Submission Types

### 1. Record Label Demos

**What labels want:**
- Private streaming link (NOT an attachment — use Dropbox, Google Drive, or private SoundCloud)
- 1-3 tracks maximum (not your entire catalog)
- Brief artist info (not your life story)
- Your streaming numbers and social proof

**Demo Email Template:**

```
Subject: Demo Submission — [Artist Name] / [Genre]

Hi [A&R Name / Label Team],

My name is [Artist Name], a [genre] artist from [city].

I have [X] unreleased tracks that I think fit [Label Name]'s sound —
specifically in the lane of [2 artists on their roster].

Private links:
1. "[Track 1]" — [private link]
2. "[Track 2]" — [private link]

Quick stats:
• [X] monthly Spotify listeners
• [Notable achievement — playlist, press, show]
• [Upcoming release/project info]

Spotify: [link]
Instagram: @[handle] ([followers])

I'd love for you to listen when you have a moment. No rush.

Thanks,
[Artist Name]
[Email] | [Phone]
```

**Rules:**
- Research the label first. Don't submit trap to a folk label.
- Find the specific A&R's name. "To whom it may concern" = instant delete.
- Keep the email under 150 words.
- NEVER attach audio files. Always use streaming links.
- Follow up ONCE after 2 weeks. Then move on.
- Submit to 20-30 labels, expect 1-3 responses.

### 2. Radio Submissions

**Types of Radio:**

| Type | How to Submit | Reach |
|---|---|---|
| **College radio** (KEXP, WFMU, KCRW) | Direct email to music director | Local/regional, high cred |
| **Online radio** (NTS, Rinse, Worldwide FM) | Email + submission forms | Global, niche audiences |
| **Commercial radio** | Requires radio promoter ($2-10K) | Mass reach, expensive |
| **Satellite radio** (SiriusXM) | Submit through distributor or label | National, SoundExchange royalties |
| **Mixshow DJs** | Direct DM/email to DJs | Scene-specific |

**College Radio Submission:**

```
Subject: New [Genre] Single for Review — [Artist Name]

Hi [Music Director Name],

I'm [Artist Name] from [city]. I have a new [genre] single called
"[Title]" that I think would be a great fit for [Station Name],
especially on [specific show name if you know it].

Stream: [link]
Download (broadcast quality WAV): [Dropbox/Google Drive link]

One sheet attached. Happy to send anything else you need.

Thanks for your time,
[Artist Name]
[Email]
```

**Finding Music Directors:**
- CMJ Directory (college radio contacts database)
- Station websites → "About" or "Contact" pages
- LinkedIn search: "[Station call letters] music director"

### 3. Blog & Publication Submissions

**Submission Platforms:**

| Platform | Cost | Response Rate | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **SubmitHub** | $1-4/submission | 48hr guaranteed response | Blogs, playlists, YouTube channels |
| **Groover** | €2/submission | 7-day guaranteed response | European blogs + radio |
| **Musosoup** | Free tier + paid | Varies | PR campaigns |
| **Direct email** | Free | 5-10% response rate | Personal connection |

**SubmitHub Tips:**
- Standard ($1) vs Premium ($2-4): Premium gets listened to faster, not necessarily accepted more
- Write a genuine note — curators say the personal message matters
- Don't submit to blogs outside your genre
- Check the blog's recent posts — are they actively publishing?

**Direct Blog Pitch:**

```
Subject: New [Genre]: [Artist Name] — "[Track Title]"

Hi [Writer/Editor Name],

I'm reaching out about coverage for my new [genre] single "[Title],"
out [date].

The track [one sentence about what makes it notable — production style,
feature, story behind it].

Private stream: [link]
Press photos + assets: [link]
EPK: [link]

I've been following [Blog Name] since [specific article or feature that
shows you actually read their site]. Would love for you to check this out.

[Artist Name]
[Links]
```

### 5. DJ Mix Show Submissions

For DJs and producers who want tracks played in mixes:

```
Subject: Promo — [Artist Name] "[Track Title]" [BPM] [Key]

Hey [DJ Name],

New [genre] track for your consideration:

"[Title]" — [BPM] BPM / [Key]
Download: [link to WAV/AIFF]
Stream: [Spotify/SoundCloud link]

Dropping [date]. Feel free to play it out — would be honored.

[Artist Name]
```

**Include:** BPM, key, and a clean WAV download link. DJs need these for mixing.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Mass blast identical emails** — every submission should feel personal
2. **Attach MP3 files** — use streaming links, never attachments
3. **Submit unfinished/rough mixes** — only send your best, fully mastered work
4. **Follow up more than once** — one follow-up after 2 weeks, then move on
5. **Get discouraged** — a 5% response rate is normal. Submit to 50, hope for 2-3
6. **Submit to wrong outlets** — a trap artist submitting to an indie folk blog = ignored
7. **Write a novel** — keep every submission under 150 words
