# Social Scheduler

Draft and schedule platform-optimized social content for music promotion.

## When to Activate

- "Post about my new single," "schedule social for release week"
- "What should I post today?" "Draft a TikTok caption"
- Release plan triggers social media phase

## Platform Guidelines

### Instagram
- **Feed posts**: Square (1080x1080) or vertical (1080x1350). 3-5x/week max.
- **Reels**: 15-90 seconds, vertical, hook in first 2 seconds. Daily during campaigns.
- **Stories**: Behind-the-scenes, polls, countdowns. Multiple daily OK.
- **Captions**: Personal, storytelling. 150-300 words. Break into paragraphs.
- **Hashtags**: 15-20 relevant tags. Mix popular (#newmusic 50M) with niche (#indieRnB 50K).
- **Best times**: 11AM-1PM, 7PM-9PM (audience timezone).

### TikTok
- **Format**: Vertical video, 15-60 seconds. Shorter = better for virality.
- **Hook**: First 2 seconds must grab attention. Start with the catchiest part of the song.
- **Caption**: Short, curiosity-driven. "This part wasn't supposed to make the album 😅"
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 max. #newmusic #unreleased #[genre] #fyp
- **Posting**: 1-3x/day during campaigns. Consistency > quality for algorithm.
- **Sound**: Use your own sound. Don't overlay trending sounds on music promotion.

### Twitter/X
- **Text posts**: Short, punchy. Questions and hot takes get engagement.
- **Media**: Attach audio snippet, artwork, or short video clip.
- **Threads**: Use for album announcements, track-by-track breakdowns.
- **Links**: Spotify/Apple Music links in replies, not main tweet (better reach).
- **Frequency**: 3-5x/day during launch week, 1-2x/day normally.

### YouTube
- **Shorts**: Vertical, <60 seconds. Repurpose TikTok content.
- **Premieres**: Use for music video drops. Schedule 24-48h ahead.
- **Community posts**: Polls, behind-the-scenes photos, release announcements.
- **SEO**: Title = "[Artist] - [Song Title] (Official Music Video/Lyric Video/Audio)"

## Content Calendar Template

For a release campaign, draft posts for each day:

```
Release Week Content Calendar
==============================

Monday (T-4):
  IG: Teaser clip (15s of hook) + "Something's coming Friday 👀"
  TikTok: Studio session clip with snippet
  X: "new one dropping this week. not ready for this conversation."

Tuesday (T-3):
  IG Story: Countdown sticker to release day
  TikTok: Behind-the-scenes of recording
  X: Share a lyric line without context

Wednesday (T-2):
  IG: Artwork reveal post + pre-save link in bio
  TikTok: "POV: you're hearing [song] for the first time" with clip
  X: Artwork + "Friday." + pre-save link

Thursday (T-1):
  IG: Carousel (artwork + lyrics + studio photo) + "tomorrow"
  TikTok: Final teaser — the climax of the song
  X: Thread — story behind the song
  Email: Send to fan list via Klaviyo

Friday (RELEASE DAY):
  IG: Full announcement post + all streaming links
  TikTok: Celebration video / reaction to it being out
  X: "it's out. link in bio. let me know your favorite part."
  IG Story: Share every fan reaction/story mention
  Email: "IT'S OUT" blast

Saturday/Sunday (Post-release):
  IG Story: Share streaming milestones, fan reactions
  TikTok: React to comments, duet fan videos
  X: Thank fans, share specific moments
```

## Drafting Rules

When drafting posts:
1. Match the artist's voice (read USER.md for their style)
2. Never use generic phrases ("link in bio!!!" "stream now!!!")
3. Create curiosity, not announcements
4. Each platform gets unique content — don't cross-post identical text
5. Include a clear CTA but make it feel natural
6. Emojis: use sparingly unless the artist's brand is emoji-heavy
