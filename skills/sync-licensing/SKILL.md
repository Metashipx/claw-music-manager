# Sync Licensing

Manage sync licensing submissions and opportunities.

## When to Activate

- "How do I get my music in a show?" "Sync licensing," "music licensing"
- "Passive income from music," "TV/film placement"
- Artist has tracks suited for visual media

## What Is Sync Licensing?

Sync = synchronization. When music is paired with visual media (TV, film, ads, games, trailers), the artist earns a sync fee + backend royalties.

**Income streams from a sync placement:**
1. **Sync fee** (upfront): $500-$500,000+ depending on the placement
2. **Master use fee**: Paid by the production to use your specific recording
3. **Performance royalties**: Collected by your PRO (ASCAP/BMI) when the show airs
4. **Re-airs and streaming**: Ongoing royalties every time the episode/ad plays

## Track Readiness Checklist

Before submitting to sync:
- [ ] You own 100% of the master recording (or have written permission from co-owners)
- [ ] Publishing splits are documented and agreed upon
- [ ] Instrumental version available
- [ ] Clean (no profanity) version available
- [ ] No uncleared samples
- [ ] Metadata complete: BPM, key, mood tags, genre, lyrics
- [ ] High-quality WAV file (24-bit/48kHz preferred for film/TV)

## Submission Platforms

| Platform | Model | Cut | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Musicbed** | Curated | 35-50% | Premium placements (ads, film) |
| **Artlist** | Subscription | Flat annual fee to artist | Volume placements |
| **Songtradr** | Marketplace | 15-30% | Broad range of opportunities |
| **Marmoset** | Curated | 50% | Boutique, high-value placements |
| **Epidemic Sound** | Work-for-hire | Buyout | Volume, but you lose ownership |
| **Musicgateway** | Marketplace | Varies | Direct with supervisors |

**Avoid**: Any platform that asks for exclusive rights to your entire catalog without guaranteed placement.

## Mood Tags for Searchability

Music supervisors search by mood. Tag your tracks accurately:

**Energy**: Energetic, Driving, Uplifting, Chill, Mellow, Dark, Intense, Dreamy
**Emotion**: Happy, Sad, Hopeful, Angry, Romantic, Nostalgic, Empowering, Anxious
**Use case**: Montage, Chase scene, Romantic scene, Party, Road trip, Documentary, Corporate, Sports

## Key Terms

- **Exclusive**: Only one entity can license the track. Higher fees, less flexibility.
- **Non-exclusive**: Multiple placements allowed. Lower per-placement fees, more opportunities.
- **Blanket license**: Covers all uses within a defined scope (e.g., all episodes of a show).
- **Gratis**: Free placement. Only accept for genuine exposure (major show, trailer).
- **Most Favored Nations (MFN)**: Both master and publishing sides get equal payment.
